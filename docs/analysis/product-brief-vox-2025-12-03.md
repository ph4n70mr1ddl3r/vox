# Product Brief: vox

**Date:** 2025-12-03
**Author:** Riddler

---

## Executive Summary

Vox is a reputation-driven web platform that enables authentic collaboration between brands, influencers, and followers by replacing vanity metrics with community-validated trust systems. Through decentralized verification, gamified reputation building, and fair marketplace dynamics, vox creates a transparent ecosystem where genuine human connections drive successful brand campaigns.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Brands waste millions on influencer partnerships undermined by fake followers and inauthentic engagement, while genuine influencers struggle to prove their value beyond follower counts. Followers want meaningful participation in brand stories but are drowned out by bots and paid actors. Current social platforms prioritize algorithmic virality over authentic human collaboration, creating a marketplace of mistrust and inefficiency.

### Problem Impact

- Brands lose 20-30% of marketing budget to fake engagement (industry estimates)
- Influencers with genuine expertise get undervalued compared to vanity accounts
- Followers miss opportunities for authentic brand collaboration and community building
- Overall ecosystem suffers from declining trust in social commerce

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Traditional platforms rely on follower counts and algorithmic recommendations that bots can easily manipulate. Verification services are expensive and don't scale. Influencer marketplaces use vanity metrics that don't reflect collaboration quality. Social platforms prioritize engagement over authenticity, creating echo chambers of fake interaction.

### Proposed Solution

Vox introduces a reputation-based collaboration platform where:
- Trust graphs connect real humans through mutual endorsements
- Decentralized voting prevents bot infiltration
- Gamified achievements make reputation building engaging
- Dynamic pricing reflects genuine reach and collaboration success
- Follower feedback creates accountability for all participants

### Key Differentiators

- Community-driven reputation vs algorithmic metrics
- Decentralized bot prevention through peer verification
- Multi-layered credibility (trust + achievements + feedback)
- Fair marketplace where quality drives value, not quantity
- Human-centric design that rewards authentic collaboration

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**Trust Graph System:**
- User registration with basic profile creation
- Trust list curation (add/remove trusted connections)
- Simple reputation scoring based on trust network connections
- Profile display of reputation metrics

**Basic Collaboration Marketplace:**
- Brand profile creation and campaign posting
- Influencer search and filtering by reputation
- Simple collaboration request and acceptance flow
- Basic messaging between brands and influencers

**Follower Verification:**
- Decentralized voting system for new user verification
- Basic bot detection through community reporting
- Trust-based access controls for premium features

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Advanced gamification and achievement badges
- Complex analytics and reporting dashboards
- Mobile app (web-only initially)
- AI-powered content moderation
- Multi-language support
- Enterprise features (API integrations, SSO)
- Advanced marketplace features (bidding, contracts)

### MVP Success Criteria

- 1,000 active users within 3 months
- 80% user satisfaction with bot prevention effectiveness
- 50 completed brand-influencer collaborations
- Average reputation score above 6.0/10
- Positive feedback on marketplace transparency
